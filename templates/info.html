Page Descriptions

  • Pokémon GO - List of all Pokémon pulled from Pokémon Database with lookups to your data in Poke Genie about Have Living Dex, Have Shiny, and shiny availability in Pokémon Go taken from Serebii, and Need on iPad account.
  • Shinies - Available shiny Pokémon in Pokémon Go and their spawn method taken from Serebii.
  • Costumes - Available costume Pokémon in Pokémon Go and their costume type, taken from Pokémon GO Fandom.
  • Forms - (Not Updated) - Available special form Pokémon like Flabébé, Spinda, Furfrou, etc., taken from Pokémon GO Wiki.
  • Rocket - Available Team Rocket shadow and purified Pokémon in Pokémon Go and their spawn method taken from Serebii.
  • All Pokémon - All Pokémon released across all games, with lookups for which ones are in Pokémon GO, taken from Wikipedia.
  • Poke Genie - Important columns taken from the Poke Genie CSV, updated when you manually export it from the Poke Genie app. Those columns are: Name, Form, Pokémon Number, Gender, CP, Quick Move, Charge Move, Charge Move 2, Scan Date, Original Scan Date, Catch Date, Lucky, Shadow/Purified, Favorite, Name (G), Name (U), Name (L).
  • Notes - View edited notes for Pokémon on the Pokémon page.
